The applet has three panels. In the leftmost, the slice of parameter space is shown. You may click within the parameter slice to change the two parameters ( and ) that vary there.

When new parameters are chosen, the other two panels will update (be patient). The middle panel shows a time series for the the resource, two prey species (with prey 2 invading), and predator. You may click in this panel to select a particular moment to be highlighted.

In the third panel, the prey and predator densities are plotted as a three-dimensional trajectory. This plot can be rotated by clicking and dragging. Also, you may press buttons 1-3 to view the dynamics in two dimensions. For example, pressing 2 will show the dynamics of prey 1 versus the predator.
