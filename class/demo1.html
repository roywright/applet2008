The applet has three panels. In the leftmost, the slice of parameter space is shown. You may click within the parameter slice to change the two parameters ( and ) that vary there. The point selected when the applet loads is the one used to produce Figure 5.

When new parameters are chosen, the other two panels will update (be patient). The middle panel shows a time series for the three prey species and invading predator. Resources are omitted for clarity. The predator is shown in violet. You may click in this panel to select a particular moment to be highlighted.

In the third panel, the three prey densities are plotted as a three-dimensional trajectory. This plot can be rotated by clicking and dragging. Also, you may press buttons 1-3 to view the dynamics in two dimensions. For example, pressing 3 will show the dynamics of prey 1 versus prey 2.
